Why Us?

We are a small family business producing toys for cats - these adorable little devils that are part of our families.

Because we Love Cats so much and having cats home we Understand Them so Well, when designing and building our products we pay maximum attention to every detail so that your cat will have the best posible toy - safety being put first!

That being said, all of our products go through a thoroughly Safety Testing Procedure in a third party test laboratory and certification body - just like in the case of products destined for children - and no product is put up for sale without first being certified as safe, so that your cat could play free of any danger.

Also, for our products we use only Premium Materials that are Natural and known to be 100% Safe for Cats.

Last but Not Least: because we are 100% Sure of the Quality of Our Products, you receive LIFETIME WARANTY!

So, in short, the benefits of choosing us are:

  • We Love Cats and understand their specific needs
  • Our products are of Premium Quality and Safety is put First

  • We only use Natural Materials which are Cat Safe
